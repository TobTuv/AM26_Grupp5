The year is 2121.

Los Angeles is drenched in neon light and smog. You wake up miles above the ground in your pod apartment for yet another day in the service of Evil Corp. Since losing your job as a console cowboy, you've become one of their many motorcycle couriers - also known as neon runners - in this jungle of skyscrapers.

It's dark, as always. The sun has been nothing more than decoration since the Golden Dome was installed nearly a hundred years ago. You bathe in the neon glow of the advertisements outside your window as you put on your riding gear. You are ready to deliver. You are ready to ride high above the city.

You are the neon runner.
