The year is 2121.

Los Angeles is drenched in neon light and smog. You wake up miles above the ground, where the city sleepers dwell, for yet another day in Evil Corp. service. Since losing your console cowboy job, you've become one of their many motorcycle couriers - also known as neon runners - in this jungle of skyscrapers.

It's dark, as always. The sun is nothing more than decoration since the Golden Dome was installed nearly a hundred years ago. You bathe in the neon glow of the advertisements outside your window as you put on your riding gear. You are ready to deliver. You are ready to ride high above the city.

You are the neon runner.
